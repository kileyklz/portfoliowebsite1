Step 1

Goals

  • My primary goal in mind while developing this website was to establish and create a professional and accessible online presence.
  • I wanted to create a user-friendly website with simple navigation and information architecture.
  • My goal was to balance between having professionalism and personality.

Design

  • I tried to design my website to be clean, minimilistic, and modern to provide a visually appealing yet user-friendly experience.
  • I chose a light pink and coral pink to be the main colors of my website.
  • For font choices, I chose Georgia and Trebuchet MS, keeping the readability easier for users.

Content

  • I included my own photography pictures taken from my digital camera and my phone, from various destinations in the world.
  • My resume highlights my skills, education, and experiences.
  • I also included videos I have taken over time.

Step 2

Information Architecture Node-Tree Diagram

My information architecture node-tree diagram can be viewed on Miro. It shows the navigation structure of my website. I color coded the local, structural, global, utility, and associative navigation. Having it color coded makes it easier to see how I've structured my website.

Step 3

Wireframes

For my website, I made wireframes in the design process and meticulously crafted them to shape the overall structure and functionality of my website. These wireframes serve as a foundation of my website, outlining the placement of key elements such as navigation menus, content sections, and interactive features. By focusing on the fundamental layout without the distraction of aesthetics, I could ensure a clear and logical user experience.

Step 4

WGAC 2.0 Accessibility

My personal website adheres to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.0, ensuring an inclusive and user-friendly experience for all visitors. I have incorporated features that make the content perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust for a wide range of individuals. All non-text content, such as images and videos, are accompanied by descriptive text alternatives. The site is navigable via keyboard, and I've taken care to provide ample time for users to consume information. The website's design prioritizes readability and consistency, making it accessible to a broad audience. By embracing these guidelines, my goal is to create an online space that caters to everyone, regardless of their abilities or disabilities.
